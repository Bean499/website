about me & my interests

whoami

I'm Bean, an 18 year old British person. I am a programmer and mathematician. I really enjoy both of these subjects because of their logical and structured nature. I'm planning on doing a degree in Mathematics & Computer Science at university.

interests

computers

As stated above, I am very interested my computers and programming. After learning some basic programming I did an A Level in computer science. It was during this time that I started to become very interested in Linux. I had never really tried it out properly, and I had only heard a few things about it, such as that it was better for programmers but very bad for gaming. It was a combination of the former and learning of dual booting that enticed me to try it out, installing Linux Mint onto a partition on my laptop.

At first I failed to see what was special about it, only really using the tools that were supplied with the distro. However, the longer time went on, the better of a fit it felt. Learning to use the terminal made my work much faster, and Valve's development of Proton let me leave my worries about game compatability behind.

Now I am an avid Linux fan, using Arch Linux on my computer daily, also being a big fan of FOSS, which my Linux experience taught me the value of.
